Tomato soup

Description

It's a very simple and tasty dish that you can make in approximately 1 hour. You can make it with ingredients you usually have at home; you will not need anything fancy.

Before you start, make sure you have a baking paper (more on that later), a blender or a hand blender, a meat of your choice, some potatoes, and cook them in advance (unless you want to eat a plain tomato soup).

Ingredients

  • A bunch of tomatoes
  • A clove of garlic
  • Dried or fresh herbs - pick one or a few
    • Rosemary
    • Sage
    • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Potatoes & meat of your choice

Steps

  1. Before cooking the tomatoes, peal potatoes and boil them. Also, cut the meat of your choice and cook it according to your preference. I usually use chicken fillets with is recipe, which I boil in a salted water. After that, make sure to cut the meat and potatoes so that it will be easier for you to eat them in a soup.
  2. Apply baking paper on a baking pan.
  3. Cut tomatoes in half, then place them on a baking sheet.
  4. Add salt to each tomato, then add a herb/s you prefer.
  5. Cut garlic and place it on each of your tomato.
  6. Add a little bit of sugar for caramelization.
  7. Cover the tomatoes with a little bit of olive oil.
  8. Bake at 230 degrees Celsius for 15 to 20, but make sure you do not burn the garlic and herbs.
  9. After baking, remove the burnt herbs, and, the most importantly, remove the skin from the baked tomatoes.
  10. Then move cleaned tomatoes and the juices that were left on a baking sheet into a cooking pot.
  11. Add some black pepper and salt to taste, then cook until it boils.
  12. Remove the pot pot the heat, and then pour the soup into a blender (or use a hand blender) to liquify it.
  13. Return it to the cooking pot, then add potatoes and meat to eat. Wait untill it bouils again
  14. You are done, and you are awesome!
