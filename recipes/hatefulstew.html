Beef stew inspired by The Hateful Eight movie

Description

A very simple, yet extremely delicious beef stew that is inspired by Quentin Tarantino's movie «The Hateful Eight»

Ingredients

  • Half of an onion
  • 220 grams of beef
  • One carrot
  • Two potatoes
  • One tablespoon of olive oil
  • Two tablespoons of flour
  • Half a teaspoon of peppercorns
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • One tablespoon of butter
  • Hot water
  • One bay leaf

Steps

  1. Chop half an onion.
  2. Cut beef as for goulash.
  3. Cut carrot into cubes.
  4. Cut potatoes into cubes (The should be slightly larger than carrots).
  5. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a saucepan.
  6. Fry an onion and then add beef to the onion.
  7. Sear the meat from all sides then add flour.
  8. Add one tablespoon of butter and stir it.
  9. Pour water into a saucepan, just to cover the meat.
  10. Add salt and peppercorns.
  11. Then add your cut potatoes and carrot
  12. Add water again to cover the ingredients with it.
  13. Use a wooden spoon to scrape off crust from the bottom of your saucepan.
  14. Cover your saucepan and simmer the stew for 30 minutes.
  15. Add bay leaf and check vegetables for softness.
  16. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  17. Enjoy the stew you just made!
